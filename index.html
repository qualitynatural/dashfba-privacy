Privacy Notice — DashFBA

Last updated: July 15, 2026

DashFBA is a private, internal business-management application developed and operated by Quality Natural Ltda, an Amazon marketplace seller based in Brazil. The application is used exclusively by our own organization to manage our own Amazon seller and advertising accounts. It is not distributed, sold, or offered to the public, and it has no external users.

What the application does

DashFBA connects to Amazon services — including the Amazon Selling Partner API and the Amazon Ads API — through authorizations that we grant to our own accounts. It retrieves business data that belongs to us, such as inventory levels, inbound shipments, product catalog details, listing prices, sales and traffic reports, and advertising campaign performance. This data is used solely for internal purposes: inventory planning, pricing analysis, and advertising management for our own products.

Data we collect from third parties

None. DashFBA does not collect, request, or process personal information from customers, visitors, or any third party. The application does not access buyer personally identifiable information. All data handled by the application refers to our own business operations.

Where data is stored

All data retrieved by DashFBA is stored locally, in a database on a single access-controlled workstation owned by Quality Natural Ltda. The application is not hosted in the cloud and exposes no public service. Authorization credentials are stored in a protected local credential store, are never shared with third parties, and are never included in source code.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share any data retrieved through Amazon services with any third party. Data is used only within our organization and only for the purposes described above.

Login with Amazon

DashFBA uses Login with Amazon (LWA) exclusively to authorize the application's access to our own Amazon accounts, under the permissions we explicitly grant. The application does not use Login with Amazon to authenticate or collect information about any other person. Authorizations can be reviewed and revoked at any time through the corresponding Amazon account settings.

Changes to this notice

If the scope of the application changes in a way that affects this notice, we will update this page and revise the date shown above.
